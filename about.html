Our Journey

More than just a store

We believe that bringing a plant into your home is the first step towards a more mindful and connected life.

Maya & Jonas

Founders of Blooming Oasis

Driven by Passion

Blooming Oasis was born in a tiny sun-drenched studio apartment in 2018.

What started as Maya’s personal obsession with rare Monsteras and Jonas’s vision for sustainable urban gardening has grown into a community of over 50,000 plant parents. We started with three simple values: Accessibility, Sustainability, and Community.

Today, we ship rare and common houseplants across the country, ensuring each one is ethically sourced and ready to thrive in its new home.
